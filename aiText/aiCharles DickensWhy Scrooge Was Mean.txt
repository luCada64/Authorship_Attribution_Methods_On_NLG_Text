In the annals of human behavior, few characters have stirred the hearts and minds of readers as Ebenezer Scrooge. Why was he so mean? What drove this man of wealth and power to disdain his fellow man so thoroughly? In this scientific study, we delve deep into the inner workings of Scrooge's mind, exploring the traumas and tragedies that shaped his hardened heart. Through an analysis of his past and present, we uncover the roots of his miserly ways and shed light on the transformative journey that led him to redemption. Join us on a Dickensian exploration of the human soul, as we seek to unravel the mystery of why Scrooge was mean.
